## Project Overview

**Multimodal Spatial Audio Toolkit** is an interactive platform for spatial audio research, immersive sound design, and accessibility-focused experiences. The toolkit integrates multimodal sensors and real-time spatialization algorithms, enabling users to explore and prototype spatial sound environments. The goal is to make a device-agnostic software for BLV user navigation.

- Multimodal sensor integration (IMU, microphones, etc.)
- Interactive demo video and headphone listening mode
